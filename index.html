Hello, my name is Jimsson. I am a web designer developer in Dublin California. My goal is to be great on what I do and become a terrific UI/UX designer. I took classes on graphic design, computer programming, psychology here and there, but all of my knowledge are self-taught. I like teaching myself and honing my skills, even though sometimes learning something new is scary and takes a long time. I do it anyways, because I believe that you can do anything you set your mind into. Most of my family are in the technology realm, so I was pulled into it as well. When designing a particular project. I love to put meaning into my design. I believe a great design should not only look pretty, but backed with meaning and convey feelings to the people looking in my work.
